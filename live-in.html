Live‑In Introductions

We introduce vetted, self‑employed live‑in companions. The companion contracts with and is paid by the client directly. The Companion Club charges a separate weekly administration fee for introduction and ongoing support.

How it works

  1. We discuss your needs and create a brief
  2. We introduce suitable companions for interview
  3. Once matched, the companion is paid directly by you
  4. Our admin fee is invoiced weekly or monthly
