What We Do

The Companion Club (operated by Almost Family Ltd) provides non‑medical home‑help and companionship. We’re an introductory service: we match you with a trusted companion and coordinate the relationship, but we do not provide or manage regulated personal care.

Brand Story

The Companion Club was created to bring warmth, trust, and genuine connection into everyday life. We provide discreet, non-medical companionship and home-help to discerning clients who value continuity, empathy, and excellence. Our brand reflects calm confidence — a timeless balance of professionalism and heart.

Typical visit activities

  • Conversation, company and social activities
  • Shopping, errands and appointment support
  • Light household help and meal preparation
  • Escorted outings and community events
Personal care: Where appropriate and privately arranged, some self‑employed companions may offer personal care directly to the client. The Companion Club does not manage or provide personal care.
